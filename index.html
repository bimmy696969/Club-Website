What is Esports?
eSports are not simply video games, but gaming at a professional level in front of spectators. Many genres of games have competitive sports, from first person shooters (FPS) such as Overwatch to multiplayer online battle arenas (MOBA) like League of Legends, and fighting games, such as the Super Smash Bros. and Street Fighter series, even have competitive scenes too. Although not all games have an existing or active eSports scene, there is potential for it more often than not.
Who are we? What do we do?
We specialize in competitive gaming and represent our school in tournaments. Our club’s goal is to bring together those who are interested in eSports or those who are already in the scene and to create opportunities, whether socially or through their competitive skill. Westview eSports fosters a competitive community for those at Westview of all skill and interest levels in eSports. Regular tournaments within school and occasional tournaments across the district are most commonplace, lending to a fun and competitive scene that many seek at Westview. We offer every opportunity we can to those who wish to learn a game and be a part of a team yet lack the ability to own the game or buy their own set up. We also offer coaching for each game for those who just don’t know how to get better or where to even start. Our rules and Code of Honor maintain an inclusive, fun, and encouraging environment for anyone. All you need is an interest to play and we’ll help guide you the rest of the way!
